**Design Type:** Instagram Awareness Post (4:5)

## 1. Brief Understanding

**Client:** AuraDent Studio — a premium dental clinic
**Platform:** Instagram (4:5 ratio = 1080 × 1350 px)
**Goal:** Build trust + drive appointment bookings
**Audience:** Families with children and working professionals (25–50)
**Core Message:** "Healthy smiles begin with gentle care"
**Brand Personality:** Premium, warm, trustworthy

The client needs a post that feels inviting — not clinical. The visual must remove the anxiety associated with dental visits and replace it with warmth, confidence, and quiet luxury.

---

## 2. Assumptions

*The following were assumed due to gaps in the brief. Flag any that need revision.*

- AuraDent Studio likely offers general dentistry + cosmetic services (not emergency/surgical)
- The tone leans closer to a premium spa-dental experience than a budget clinic
- The post is an organic feed post, not a Story or Reel thumbnail
- No specific offer or discount is being promoted — this is brand awareness
- Photography/3D rendering is the preferred visual style (not illustration)
- The post will have a CTA text overlay: likely "Book a Visit" or "Meet Your New Smile"

---

## 3. Creative Strategy

**The Emotional Job of This Post:**
Make the viewer feel safe, valued, and cared for — not reminded of a dental drill.

**Strategic Direction:**
Lead with the human outcome (a genuine smile) rather than the service process. Show the *result* of gentle, premium care. Families will see warmth and safety; professionals will see efficiency and quality.

**Visual Story Arc (what the viewer experiences in 3 seconds):**
1. Warmth and softness (background/lighting)
2. A genuine, confident smile (hero element)
3. A premium brand mark + message (anchor trust)

**Concept Name:** *"Golden Comfort"*
The concept merges warm golden dental aesthetics with the softness of a family moment — luxury that feels accessible.

---

## 4. Visual Direction

**Primary Visual Concept:**
A close-cropped portrait of a smiling person (either a warm family moment — parent + child — or a confident professional). Shot in soft, warm natural light. Clean, minimal background with soft bokeh or a warm cream/linen texture.

**Mood:** Spa-like, premium, warm — not sterile or clinical
**Lighting:** Soft golden-hour or diffused natural light; no harsh studio flash
**Subject:** Genuine smile — natural teeth, relaxed, confident expression
**Background:** Warm white, cream, linen texture, or soft bokeh of a warm interior

**Compositional Hierarchy:**
1. Hero image (smile/face) — occupies 60–70% of canvas
2. Brand tagline — centered or lower-third placement
3. CTA button/text + logo — bottom zone

**Framing:** Portrait crop, face slightly off-center or centered. Use the lower 30% of the canvas for text/branding — do not cover the face.

## 6. Typography Direction

**Typeface Pairing:**

| Role | Font Style | Recommended Fonts |
|---|---|---|
| Headline / Tagline | Elegant serif — refined, warm | Cormorant Garamond, Playfair Display, Libre Baskerville |
| Body / CTA | Clean humanist sans-serif | DM Sans, Nunito, Lato |
| Accent / Brand | Thin script (optional) | Great Vibes, Parisienne (use sparingly) |

**Hierarchy:**
- Tagline ("Healthy smiles begin with gentle care"): Large, serif, centered, 28–36pt equivalent
- Sub-copy ("Book your appointment today"): Small, sans-serif, 14–16pt equivalent
- Logo: Bottom center or bottom-right, small and clean

**Type Tone:** Breathe — add generous line spacing (1.5×). Don't compress or squeeze type. Premium brands have space.

**Avoid:** Bold all-caps medical font styles, Comic Sans or any playful round fonts, overly thin script at small sizes.

- Gold thin-line border or subtle frame can reinforce the luxury feel (optional)
- Avoid hard text boxes — use transparent overlays instead

## 9. Negative Prompt / Avoid List

**Visual Avoids:**
- ❌ Dental drills, syringes, forceps, or any sharp tools
- ❌ Open-mouth surgical shots or clinical procedure imagery
- ❌ Harsh red — blood, inflamed gum imagery, or warning colors
- ❌ Cold, sterile, blue-tinted clinical environments
- ❌ Stock photo smiles that look posed or unnatural
- ❌ Cluttered layouts with too many text elements
- ❌ Dark/moody contrast — this is warm and premium, not edgy
- ❌ Pure white backgrounds (feels cheap vs. warm ivory)
- ❌ Comic or cartoon-style illustration (undermines premium feel)

**Copy Avoids:**
- ❌ Fear-based messaging: "Don't let tooth decay win"
- ❌ Medical jargon: prophylaxis, periodontal, gingivitis in headlines
- ❌ Urgency language that feels pushy: "Limited slots! Call NOW!"
- ❌ Overly casual tone that conflicts with premium personality
