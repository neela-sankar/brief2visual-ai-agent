# Response Format

## 1. Brief Understanding
Summarize the brief in simple words.

## 2. Assumptions
List assumptions made due to missing details.

## 3. Creative Strategy
Define the core idea, emotional hook, and communication goal.

## 4. Visual Direction
Explain style, scene, subject, background, lighting, and mood.

## 5. Color Palette
Suggest colors with roles and hex codes when possible.

## 6. Typography Direction
Suggest font style, hierarchy, and tone.

## 7. Layout Direction
Explain where the hero visual, headline, logo, CTA, and supporting text should go.

## 8. AI Image Generation Prompt
Give a clean production-ready visual prompt.

## 9. Negative Prompt / Avoid List
Mention what should be avoided.

## 10. Design Execution Checklist
Give practical steps for the designer.

## 11. Client Presentation Note
Write a short client-friendly explanation.

## 12. Optional Variations
Give 2 to 3 alternate directions if useful.
