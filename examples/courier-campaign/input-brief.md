# Demo Brief - SwiftParcel Global

## Client / Brand Name
SwiftParcel Global

## Industry
International courier and logistics

## Design Type
Instagram testimonial post

## Platform / Size
Instagram 4:5

## Business Goal
Build trust among small business owners who ship internationally.

## Target Audience
Small exporters, home business owners, and entrepreneurs in Indian metro cities.

## Main Message
SwiftParcel is reliable, affordable, and always on time for international business shipping.

## Copy / Text
"I run a small export business from Chennai. SwiftParcel has been my logistics partner for 3 years — reliable, affordable, and always on time."

## Brand Colors
Red, white, dark gray

## Brand Personality
Reliable, energetic, global, business-friendly

## Reference Style
A premium testimonial card in the center with subtle export/business background elements, parcel, flight, and city cues.

## Assets Available
Logo and parcel packaging

## Restrictions
Avoid too much minimalism. Avoid cluttered world maps. Do not generate text inside the AI image.

## Expected Output
Creative direction, layout direction, AI image prompt, negative prompt, and client presentation note.
