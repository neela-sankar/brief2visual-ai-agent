## 3. CREATIVE STRATEGY

**Big Idea:**
*Speed as a promise, not just a feature.*

The visual should make the viewer feel movement — a parcel being lifted off by the world's most powerful force: a commercial jet. Red amplifies urgency and energy. The composition should feel like a motion poster, not a static ad.

**Emotional Trigger:**
Trust + Excitement. The customer should feel: *"My parcel is in powerful hands."*

**Visual Metaphor:**
A premium parcel box soaring above the clouds, with a jet aircraft cutting through red atmospheric haze in the background — symbolising global reach and unstoppable delivery speed.

## 4. VISUAL DIRECTION

**Concept Name:** *"Above the Clouds"*

**Scene Description:**
- A beautifully lit premium parcel box (kraft brown with red branding tape/label) floats or angles dramatically in the foreground
- A commercial jet aircraft is visible in the mid-ground, cutting through soft red-tinted clouds
- The sky transitions from deep crimson at the top to a warm orange-red at the horizon
- Subtle motion blur on the jet to imply speed
- Cinematic lighting — a god-ray or lens flare from the sun behind the clouds
- Overall mood: editorial, premium, high-end logistics brand

**Depth & Atmosphere:**
- Foreground: Parcel (sharp focus, 3D product feel)
- Mid-ground: Aircraft (slight motion blur)
- Background: Red sky + clouds (soft, atmospheric)

**Visual Style Reference Mood:**
Think: DHL premium campaign meets Emirates cargo aesthetics meets Apple product photography lighting

## 9. NEGATIVE PROMPT / AVOID LIST

**Visually Avoid:**
- ❌ Flat, lifeless red (use rich, dark crimson — not fire-engine red)
- ❌ Clipart-style parcel or aircraft illustrations
- ❌ Overcrowded composition with too many elements
- ❌ Low-contrast text (red text on red — invisible and unprofessional)
- ❌ Centered layout with no visual tension
- ❌ Stock photo-style airplane on plain blue sky (too generic)
- ❌ Cheap gradient backgrounds without depth
- ❌ Too many fonts (max 2 font families)
- ❌ Neon red or overly saturated tones (kills premium feel)
- ❌ Cartoon or comic visual styles

**Messaging Avoid:**
- ❌ Vague claims like "We deliver fast" — say *how* fast or *where*
- ❌ Overcrowded copy — one tagline maximum on the post visual
- ❌ All-caps body text (hard to read at small sizes)
