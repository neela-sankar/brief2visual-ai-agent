# Visual Style Library

## Premium Minimal
Use when the brand needs elegance, trust, or high value.
Traits: negative space, refined type, soft shadows, controlled palette.

## Warm Human
Use when the brand needs emotional connection.
Traits: real people, warm light, natural scenes, soft expressions.

## Bold Campaign
Use for strong announcements and social campaigns.
Traits: large typography, high contrast, powerful subject, energetic composition.

## Clean Corporate
Use for service businesses, B2B, professional brands.
Traits: structured grids, balanced spacing, clear CTA, neat icons.

## Playful 3D
Use for education, kids, apps, friendly brands.
Traits: rounded forms, soft lighting, expressive characters, bright palette.

## Editorial Lifestyle
Use for fashion, beauty, premium products.
Traits: photography-led layout, stylish cropping, strong type pairing.

## Product Hero
Use for product ads.
Traits: product as main subject, controlled lighting, benefit-led composition.

## Festival Subtle
Use for festival greetings without making it too decorative.
Traits: cultural cues, warm glow, symbolic elements, brand-safe styling.
