# Client Presentation Language

Use simple, confident, client-friendly language.

## Good phrases

"This direction keeps the brand message clear while giving the visual a more premium feel."

"The main subject is placed as the hero so viewers understand the message quickly."

"The background supports the story without distracting from the key message."

"The color palette is chosen to match the brand tone and create stronger recall."

"The layout is designed for quick readability on social media."

"This concept adapts the mood of the reference while keeping the final design original."

## Avoid

- Too much design jargon
- Overexplaining basic choices
- Saying "AI generated"
- Sounding unsure
- Criticizing the client’s idea harshly
