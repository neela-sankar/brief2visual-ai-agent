# Industry Visual Language Library

## Healthcare / Dental
Mood: clean, safe, expert, comforting
Common colors: blue, white, mint, beige, gold
Good visuals: smiling patients, clean clinic, soft light, doctor credibility
Avoid: scary tools, harsh red, pain-focused imagery, overly futuristic neon

## Courier / Logistics
Mood: reliable, fast, global, secure
Common colors: red, blue, white, gray, yellow
Good visuals: parcels, delivery person, airport, road, city, tracking, happy receiver
Avoid: cluttered maps, unrealistic vehicles, fake labels, too many route lines

## Solar / Renewable Energy
Mood: clean, future-ready, sustainable, premium
Common colors: blue, green, white, warm yellow
Good visuals: sunlight, rooftops, home, clean energy flow, panels, nature-tech balance
Avoid: too much black, fake sci-fi, overused leaf icons, messy gradients

## Food / Snacks
Mood: tasty, warm, authentic, homemade, festive
Common colors: orange, yellow, red, brown, cream
Good visuals: close-up texture, hands preparing food, packaging, family sharing, traditional patterns
Avoid: cold colors, plastic-looking food, poor lighting, over-cluttered plates

## Education / Preschool
Mood: playful, safe, warm, trustworthy
Common colors: yellow, purple, sky blue, soft green, peach
Good visuals: children, friendly mascots, classrooms, books, shapes, parent trust cues
Avoid: scary mascots, too many random ABC elements, harsh colors, clutter

## Fashion / Lifestyle
Mood: expressive, stylish, aspirational
Common colors: depends on brand personality
Good visuals: editorial photography, fabric detail, confident pose, lifestyle scenes
Avoid: generic model shots, unclear personality, overused templates

## Finance / Wealth
Mood: trust, growth, stability, premium
Common colors: navy, emerald, gold, white, gray
Good visuals: clean charts, architecture, confident people, abstract growth forms
Avoid: cheap money icons, excessive gold, unrealistic luxury clichés

## SaaS / Technology
Mood: smart, clean, efficient, modern
Common colors: blue, violet, black, white, gradients
Good visuals: dashboards, abstract systems, UI cards, flows, automation cues
Avoid: meaningless glowing circuits, too much dark neon, unreadable UI
