# Typography Direction Guide

## Typography Roles
Heading: Personality and impact
Body: Readability and clarity
Accent: Highlights, labels, small emphasis

## Font Style Directions

### Premium
Elegant serif or refined modern sans.
Use high spacing, controlled weights, and clean hierarchy.

### Modern Tech
Geometric sans or neutral sans.
Use clear weights, sharp alignment, and UI-friendly spacing.

### Friendly Brand
Rounded sans or soft humanist sans.
Use approachable weight and generous spacing.

### Bold Campaign
Condensed bold sans or heavy display type.
Use strong contrast and short headlines.

### Traditional / Cultural
Serif or soft display style with warm supporting sans.
Avoid making it too decorative or hard to read.

## Rules
- Typography should support the message
- Do not use too many font styles
- Make hierarchy obvious
- Avoid decorative fonts for long text
