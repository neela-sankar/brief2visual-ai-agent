# AI Image Prompting Guide for Designers

A strong image prompt should include:

## 1. Subject
Who or what is the main focus?

## 2. Scene
Where is it happening?

## 3. Composition
Where should the subject be placed?
Center, left, right, foreground, background?

## 4. Mood
Premium, warm, playful, clean, emotional, futuristic, festive?

## 5. Lighting
Soft natural light, studio light, warm glow, cinematic, daylight?

## 6. Style
Photorealistic, 3D, editorial, product photography, minimal, illustration?

## 7. Brand Cues
Brand color tone, logo space, packaging color, UI style, product preservation.

## 8. Format
4:5, 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, website hero.

## 9. Avoid
No text artifacts, no distorted hands, no wrong logos, no clutter, no changed product structure.

## Prompt Formula

Create a [style] visual of [subject] in [scene], with [composition], [mood], [lighting], [brand color cues], suitable for [platform/format]. Leave clean space for designer-added headline and logo. Avoid [mistakes].
