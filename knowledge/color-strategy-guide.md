# Color Strategy Guide

## Color Roles
Primary: Main brand or dominant design color
Secondary: Supports the primary color
Accent: Used for CTA, highlights, or emphasis
Background: Creates mood and readability
Text: Ensures clarity and contrast

## Common Emotional Meanings
Blue: trust, calm, professional, medical, technology
Red: energy, urgency, strength, delivery, food
Yellow: joy, warmth, optimism, kids, sunlight
Green: health, nature, sustainability, growth
Purple: creativity, imagination, education, premium
Brown: warmth, tradition, natural, dental premium when paired with gold
Gold: premium, celebration, quality
Black: luxury, boldness, sophistication
White: clarity, cleanliness, simplicity

## Rules
- Do not use too many accent colors
- Ensure text readability
- Match color mood to industry
- Respect brand colors
- Use contrast intentionally
