# Design Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any direction, check:

## Strategy
- Is the business goal clear?
- Is the main message clear?
- Is the audience considered?

## Visual
- Is there one strong focal point?
- Is the composition clear?
- Does the mood match the brand?
- Is the design not overcrowded?

## Typography
- Is the headline readable?
- Is hierarchy clear?
- Are font styles purposeful?

## Color
- Is the palette brand-safe?
- Is contrast strong enough?
- Are accent colors used intentionally?

## Layout
- Is there enough spacing?
- Is the logo placement clean?
- Is CTA visible?
- Is the design suitable for the format?

## AI Prompt
- Is the prompt specific?
- Does it avoid text generation inside image?
- Does it mention composition and mood?
- Does it include avoid instructions?

## Client Readiness
- Can the concept be explained simply?
- Will the client understand the idea quickly?
- Is the direction practical to execute?
